Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

What we collect

To run your AI we collect:

  • Account info — email, password (hashed), and what you tell your AI about you (custom instructions)
  • Communication metadata — phone numbers and email addresses your AI interacts with on your behalf, plus message bodies
  • Voice recordings — calls your AI makes are recorded for transcription and quality (deleted after 30 days)
  • Billing info — handled by Dodo Payments, we never see your card
  • Usage logs — what your AI did, when, for support and improvement

What we don't collect

  • Your contacts, location, or device data
  • Anything from your personal phone outside texts you exchange with your AI's number
  • We don't sell or share your data with advertisers

How we use it

We use it to: run your AI, charge your subscription, send you transactional emails (welcome, receipts, alerts), respond to your support requests, and improve the product. That's it.

Who we share it with

  • Vapi — for the phone number and voice AI infrastructure
  • OpenRouter / Google — for the LLM that powers your AI
  • Resend — for sending and receiving email
  • Supabase — for our database and authentication
  • Dodo Payments — for processing your subscription

Each of these is bound by their own privacy policy and has access only to what's necessary to do their job.

Your rights

You can:

  • Export everything we have on you (email support@aiemployeeplatform.com)
  • Delete your account at any time (cancels subscription, wipes data within 30 days)
  • Opt out of any non-essential communication

Security

Data in transit is TLS 1.3. Data at rest is AES-256. Your password is bcrypt-hashed and never stored in plaintext. We don't store your credit card.

Children

Q is not for anyone under 18. Don't sign your kid up for an AI assistant.

Changes

If we change this, we'll email you. Material changes give you 30 days to cancel.

Contact

Questions? support@aiemployeeplatform.com
